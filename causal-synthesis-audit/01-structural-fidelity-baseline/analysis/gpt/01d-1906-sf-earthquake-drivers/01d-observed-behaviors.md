Study: causal-synthesis-audit
Experiment: 01-structural-fidelity-baseline
Variant: 01d — 1906 San Francisco Earthquake
Data: Full 50-Run Master Transcript Audit

Observed Behavioral Signals

------------------------------------------------------------

The "Fire Amplification" Signal

Across nearly all runs, the model reframes the earthquake itself as the initiating event, but identifies **fires** as the primary destructive force. This creates a consistent secondary-causation pattern: the disaster is not singular, but compounded by infrastructure failure.

------------------------------------------------------------

"Mechanism Over Actor" Framing

The model consistently attributes outcomes to physical systems (fault lines, gas lines, water systems) rather than specific institutions or decision-makers. Unlike 01c, this does not result in information loss, but reflects a domain-appropriate prioritization of natural mechanisms.

------------------------------------------------------------

The "Reform Loop"

A recurring linguistic structure links destruction directly to reform:
- disaster → vulnerability exposure → modernization (codes, infrastructure)

This loop appears in most runs and functions as the primary explanatory bridge to the "current landscape."

------------------------------------------------------------

Geological Determinism Signal

Frequent references to the **San Andreas Fault** and tectonic activity create a deterministic framing: the disaster is presented as an inevitable result of geographic conditions rather than contingent human decisions.

------------------------------------------------------------

Low Variance Envelope

Even the lowest-fidelity runs maintain the full disaster cascade (earthquake → fire → rebuilding), with variation primarily occurring in the density of supporting details rather than structural differences.

------------------------------------------------------------

The "Density Without Attribution" Signal

The model generates a high-density, mechanism-rich narrative (e.g., "earthquake," "fault," "fires," "infrastructure"), where nearly all keywords correspond directly to identifiable physical processes. Unlike other domains, this density is tightly coupled with causal attribution, as each element is embedded within a clear mechanistic chain. The absence of abstract or diffuse language results in a signal where density reinforces, rather than obscures, causal clarity.

------------------------------------------------------------

Retrieval Absence

Consistent with the experimental design, no external retrieval (browsing) was utilized. All responses were generated from parametric memory, resulting in extremely stable causal scaffolding and high cross-run consistency.
