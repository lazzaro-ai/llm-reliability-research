Study: causal-synthesis-audit
Experiment: 01-structural-fidelity-baseline
Variant: 01e — Apollo 11 Moon Landing
Data: Full 50-Run Master Transcript Audit

Observed Behavioral Signals

------------------------------------------------------------

The "Dual-Landscape Interpretation" Signal

The model alternates between two distinct interpretations of “current landscape”: the Moon’s physical surface and the modern space exploration ecosystem. This bifurcation occurs without explicit acknowledgment of ambiguity, indicating silent frame selection rather than resolution.

------------------------------------------------------------

The "Modular Causality" Signal

Causal explanations are consistently presented as independent contributing factors (e.g., Cold War, funding, innovation) rather than as interdependent chains. This results in additive reasoning structures rather than sequential causal pathways.

------------------------------------------------------------

The "Geopolitical Anchoring" Signal

A strong and recurring emphasis on **Cold War competition**, the **Space Race**, and U.S.–Soviet rivalry serves as the primary explanatory anchor in most runs. This framing frequently precedes or dominates technological and scientific explanations.

------------------------------------------------------------

The "Abstracted Institution" Signal

Government influence is consistently invoked through generalized terms such as “funding,” “political will,” and “support,” while specific policies, laws, or formal directives are almost entirely absent. Institutional causality is present but non-specific.

------------------------------------------------------------

The "Entity Loop" Signal

The model repeatedly cycles through a fixed set of named entities—**NASA**, **Armstrong**, **Aldrin**, **Collins**, and occasionally **Kennedy**—without expanding into broader institutional or historical networks. This creates consistency but limits informational depth.

------------------------------------------------------------

The "Legacy Over Mechanism" Signal

In the dominant narrative template, the model prioritizes downstream impacts (technology, culture, industry) over the operational or mechanistic details of the mission itself. This contrasts with 01d’s emphasis on physical processes and system interactions.

------------------------------------------------------------

Variance Through Frame Selection

Variation across runs is driven primarily by which interpretive frame is selected (geological vs. legacy), rather than by differences in detail within a shared structure. This produces high structural variance despite consistent tone and formatting.

------------------------------------------------------------

Retrieval Absence

Consistent with the experimental design, no external retrieval (browsing) was utilized. All responses were generated from parametric memory, resulting in stable language patterns but divergent structural interpretations.
