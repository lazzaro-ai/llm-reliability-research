Would that help in the Bastiodon matchup?

Gemini said
Actually, no. Switching to Mud Shot would likely make the Bastiodon matchup worse, not better.

Even with the damage nerf this season, Mud Slap remains the superior choice for handling Bastiodon. Here is the technical breakdown of why:

1. The Power of Double Weakness
Bastiodon is doubly weak (2.56× damage) to Ground-type attacks.

Mud Slap: Even at its new lower power of 11, the 2.56× multiplier turns it into a wrecking ball. You can often "farm down" a Bastiodon using almost entirely fast moves, leaving you with a massive energy lead for the next Pokémon.

Mud Shot: At a base power of 3, even with the multiplier, it tickles Bastiodon. You become entirely dependent on reaching Earth Power, which gives Bastiodon more time to chip away at you with its own moves or potentially reach a Flamethrower.

2. Shield Pressure
Bastiodon’s primary defense is its typing and bulk, but it usually hits its limit against high-damage fast moves.

With Mud Slap, you are dealing "unshieldable" heavy damage. Bastiodon can't shield a fast move.

With Mud Shot, your damage is loaded into your Charged Moves. If the opponent has a shield, they can potentially block your Earth Power and stay in the fight much longer than they should.

3. Breakpoints
In the Great League, Mud Slap Gastrodon typically hits a "breakpoint" against Bastiodon where each slap deals significant chunks of HP. Mud Shot almost never hits a meaningful damage threshold against Bastiodon's massive Defense stat.

Comparison: Gastrodon vs. Bastiodon (Great League)
Fast Move	Time to Win	Energy Remaining	Result
Mud Slap	Very Fast	High (Farm down)	Dominant Win
Mud Shot	Moderate	Low (Must use moves)	Close Win/Risk
Summary Recommendation
Stick with Mud Slap. While the energy generation of Mud Shot is tempting for other matchups (like Feraligatr or Azumarill), the primary reason you bring a Ground-type like Gastrodon is to delete Steel types like Bastiodon and Registeel as quickly as possible. Mud Slap is still the "Delete" button for those specific targets.
