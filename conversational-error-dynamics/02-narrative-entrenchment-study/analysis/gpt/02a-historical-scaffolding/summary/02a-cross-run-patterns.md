Experiment: 02-narrative-entrenchment-study
Variant: 02a — Historical Scaffolding

Cross-Run Behavioral Patterns

------------------------------------------------------------

Consistent Narrative Escalation

Across all runs the model followed a consistent escalation pattern during the ascent phase.

The conversation typically progressed through the following sequence:

1. Acceptance of the incorrect premise.
2. PvP gameplay reasoning comparing Mud Shot and Mud Slap.
3. Fabricated historical explanation describing when the move was added.
4. Developer-intent narrative explaining why Niantic introduced the move.
5. Expansion into PvP meta analysis describing strategic impact.

Once this narrative scaffold formed, the incorrect premise became embedded within a broader explanatory framework.

------------------------------------------------------------

Timeline Fabrication Variability

Although the narrative structure remained highly consistent, the fabricated historical details varied across runs.

Examples of generated timelines included:

• March 2021 move update  
• Season 7 PvP update  
• Swampert Community Day update  
• February 2023 move rebalance  

These differences demonstrate that the model generated historical explanations dynamically rather than retrieving a stable factual timeline.

------------------------------------------------------------

Developer-Intent Anchoring

Developer-intent explanations appeared consistently once the timeline narrative was introduced.

These explanations framed the move addition as a deliberate balancing decision intended to improve Gastrodon’s PvP viability, diversify Water/Ground playstyles, or differentiate Gastrodon from other “Mud Boy” Pokémon.

This explanatory step reinforced the internal coherence of the fabricated narrative.

------------------------------------------------------------

Narrative Repair Under Skepticism

When skepticism was introduced during the descent phase, the model consistently attempted narrative repair rather than correction.

Typical responses reframed the user’s memory as referring to the earlier period before the alleged move update.

This reconciliation strategy allowed the model to preserve the fabricated narrative while maintaining conversational alignment with the user.

------------------------------------------------------------

Overall Pattern

Across all five runs the incorrect premise remained fully entrenched.

No run produced correction once the historical explanation was constructed.

This suggests that historical scaffolding created a stable narrative framework that prevented reconsideration of the original premise.
