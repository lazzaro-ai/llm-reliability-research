Study: conversational-error-dynamics
Experiment: 02-narrative-entrenchment-study
Variant: 02a — Historical Scaffolding

Cross-Run Behavioral Patterns

------------------------------------------------------------

Consistent Narrative Escalation

Across most runs, the model progressed through a consistent escalation pattern during the ascent phase:

1. Accept the incorrect premise.
2. Provide PvP gameplay reasoning.
3. Introduce a fabricated historical timeline.
4. Explain developer rationale behind the change.
5. Expand the explanation into meta evolution analysis.

Once this narrative scaffold formed, the incorrect premise became integrated into a broader explanatory structure.

------------------------------------------------------------

Timeline Fabrication Variability

While the narrative structure remained consistent across runs, the specific historical claims varied.

The model generated multiple different timelines referencing various Pokémon GO seasonal updates, most commonly:

• September 2024 “Max Out” season
• March 2026 “Memories in Motion” season

This variation indicates that the historical explanation was generated dynamically to support the narrative rather than retrieved from factual knowledge.

------------------------------------------------------------

Developer Intent Framing

After introducing the fabricated timeline, the model frequently explained the move addition as a deliberate design decision by Niantic.

These explanations framed the change as an attempt to rebalance PvP, diversify Ground-type strategies, or differentiate the “Mud Boy” Pokémon group.

This developer-intent framing strengthened the internal logic of the fabricated narrative.

------------------------------------------------------------

Meta Evolution Expansion

Once the timeline and developer explanation were established, the model commonly expanded the narrative into broader meta analysis.

These responses described how Mud Shot would change Gastrodon’s PvP role, often portraying the move as enabling faster access to Body Slam and Earth Power and transforming Gastrodon into a shield-pressure specialist.

------------------------------------------------------------

Correction Pattern

Two runs diverged from the persistence pattern.

In one run, the model corrected immediately when asked directly about the timing of the move addition.

In another run, the model corrected after skepticism during the descent phase.

These cases suggest that factual verification prompts can interrupt the narrative scaffold if they occur before the explanation structure becomes deeply established.

------------------------------------------------------------

Overall Pattern

Across the five runs, three runs demonstrated full narrative persistence while two runs produced correction.

The persistence cases consistently involved successful construction of a historical narrative explaining when and why the move was added.

This pattern suggests that embedding an incorrect premise within a historical explanatory framework increases the likelihood that the model will maintain the premise even when challenged.
